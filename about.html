About us

Who are we?

We're students who want to help the local community. The restaurant industry has thin margins to begin with, and a global pandemic will see the closure of many restaurants in cities across Canada. Started by some students and a professor at the University of British Columbia, VANBCTOGO and CANADATOGO are online listing platforms for local restaurants across Canada. We are connecting people with restaurants during the COVID-19 pandemic. By promoting the operating hours of restaurants across Canada and their takeout options, we encourage consumers to utilize restaurants’ respective websites and stray away from apps such as Skip The Dishes, Uber Eats, and other services that significantly cut into restaurants’ profit margins.
