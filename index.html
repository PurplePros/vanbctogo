A simple listing to help make an impact

The change in people's daily schedules and mobility has affected restaurants as they try to find ways to adapt. We started out by making a simple list of local restaurants in Vancouver and the options they offer. Now, we've expanded our platform to canadatogo.ca and have a database of 18 cities across Canada. As restaurant owners and goers revert back to dining in, we're looking to help people do it safely. Our latest feature is a map that displays the current popularity of a restaurant based on either live or historical data.

Let's all show our support for the hard-working owners, servers, cooks, and everyone else in the industry.
